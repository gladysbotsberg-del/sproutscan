## Cost Scaling Projections

| Users | Monthly Hosting | Monthly AI | Total Monthly |
|-------|-----------------|------------|---------------|
| 100 | $0 | $5 | $5 |
| 1,000 | $0 | $20-30 | $20-30 |
| 5,000 | $20-45 | $50-100 | $70-145 |
| 10,000 | $45-70 | $100-200 | $145-270 |
| 50,000 | $100-200 | $300-500 | $400-700 |

**Break-even math:**
- 50 users at $7.99/mo = $400/mo revenue
- Covers costs at ~5,000 user scale
- Everything beyond = profit

## Hidden Costs to Watch

| Risk | Potential Cost | Mitigation |
|------|----------------|------------|
| AI API overuse | $$$$ | Pre-generate explanations, cache aggressively |
| App Store rejection | Time, not money | Follow guidelines carefully |
| Database scaling | $25-100/mo | Monitor, optimize queries |
| Trademark issues | $500+ | Research name thoroughly before committing |

## Capital Required

**To get MVP live:** $200-300

**To run for 6 months while growing:** $500-1,000

**Comfortable cushion for unexpected:** $1,500

**Recommendation:** Budget $1,000 total. You likely won't spend it all, but it removes any friction.
